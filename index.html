Dr. Stone

After the sudden mass petrification of Earth, science prodigy Senku Ishigami finds himself awake in a new Stone World alongside his musclehead best friend Taiju Oki. Together, through science, Senku and Taiju wish to reverse the petrification of humanity and rebuild Earth in one leap, from the Stone Age to the Modern World!
